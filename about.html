About

I am a Fashion Technology student working at the intersection of textiles, electronics, and human-centred design. My interests include smart fabrics, wearable sensing, apparel CAD, and sustainable materials. I enjoy turning lab experiments into clear prototypes and publishable case studies.

Strengths: pattern engineering, BOM planning, rapid prototyping, fabric testing, and visual documentation. I collaborate with teams to bridge craft and computation.
